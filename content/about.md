## About Me

I am Muhammed Adeyemi Adedigba, an engineering professional with a passion for innovative software solutions that benefit the AECO industry, especially in relation to enabling engineering simulation at scale.

I have an excellent background in computational mechanics, static/dynamic finite element structural analysis, engineering design, and a strong zeal for software development. My formal engineering education culminates with a joint master's degree in Advanced Solid Mechanics, coordinated by engineering schools and industrial partners across France, Greece, and Italy. I hold a Bachelor's degree in Civil Engineering (Structural Engineering specialization).

## What I Do

I currently work as a Research and Development Engineer at Ansys (part of Synopsys), based in Lyon, France, where I contribute to various open-source activities relating to the [PyAnsys](https://docs.pyansys.com/) ecosystem.
