## Research Experience

**Development of a Conceptual Design Software for OWT Jackets — Master's Thesis**

Conducted review of renewable energy trends and offshore wind foundation technologies; developed a Python-based jacket foundation pre-sizing software to serve as the jacket model generation module of the full conceptual design software; established structural analysis and design workflows in SACS considering ultimate and fatigue limit states; laid the groundwork for future integration of the jacket generation module with the SACS Analysis Engine by defining the requirements of Python modules for automated analyses launch, automatic member redesign, and custom post-processing of SACS analyses results.

**Optimal Design of an Offshore Jacket Platform — Bachelor's Thesis**

Conducted bibliographic research; acquired jacket platform design data typical of Nigerian oil fields; carried out size optimisation of structural members under gravity, wind, and hydrodynamic loads; produced improved design through 29% reduction in platform weight; performed results validation with those from literature.

*Publication: M.A. Adedigba and A.A. Adedeji (2021). [Optimal Design of an Offshore Jacket Platform](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1q28KamsFo8YMyLr1P_FHN_TDRt16cs7Z/view?usp=sharing), Journal of Science and Engineering Production — Unilorin Science and Engineering Periodicals (USEP-JSEP)*
